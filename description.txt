This toolkit provides a collection of hail retrieval techniques for weather radar data built upon Py-ART and col_processing. Hail retrievals include (1) Hail Size Discrimination Algorithm - HSDA (Ortega et al. 2016), (2) Hail Differential Reflectivity - HDR (Depue et al. 2007), (3) Maximum Expected Size of Hail - MESH (Witt et al. 1998). Dual Pol corrections include (1) SNR calculation from radiosonde using Py-ART calculate_snr_from_reflectivity, (2) Unfolding of PHIDP using Py-ART get_phidp_unf, (3) Recalculation of KDP using CSU radartools Bringi technique, (4) Correction of ZDR attenuation using Bringi relationship (KDP), (5) Correction of DBZH attenuation using Py-ART calculate_attenuation, (6) Correction of RHOHV from noise (Schuur et al. 2003 NOAA report (p7 eq 5)), (7) Correction of ZDR from noise (Schuur et al. 2003 NOAA report (p7 eq 6)). Notebooks are provided to apply the listed corrections and methods, and for visualisation of a single volume.
